Trivia

They Understand Space and Time

A 2017 study found pigeons could evaluate the time it takes a digital line to be drawn according to its length.

They are Excellent at Homing

Pigeons can find their way home from 1300 miles away even if they've been transported in complete isolation.

They can diagnose cancer

A 2015 study revealed that pigeons can be trained to diagnose cancer from images with astonishing accuracy of 99%.
